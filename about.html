Our story

One roof for
the whole barnyard.

Animal Health Center started with a simple idea: the folks of Southwest Minnesota shouldn't need three different stops for their dog's food, their cat's litter, and their cattle's vaccines. So we put it all under one roof — a full pet & livestock store, a pet resort for boarding and doggy daycare, on-staff grooming, and a large-animal veterinarian.

In 2020, longtime team member Theresa bought the store she'd helped run for years — and kept it exactly what it's always been: local, friendly, and ready for animals big and small. If we don't have it, we'll order it in.
